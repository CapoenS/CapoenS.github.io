About Deep Time

Deep Time is an interactive timeline that runs from the formation of the Earth all the way to the present day. It's a passion project that also doubles as my portfolio, click the glowing ME marker at the present-day end of the timeline to visit my personal page.

Usage

  1. Navigate: Scroll to zoom, drag to pan. Pressing Reset view resets your view to default.
  2. Time units: Click any eon, era or period to focus it and open its info panel. Jump to opens a panel that lets you select a period.
  3. Species: The coloured bars are species. Click the species label to open an info card. Hide species toggles them.
  4. Events: Amber diamonds mark big moments like impacts and ice ages. Hover over them to see details. Hide events toggles them.
  5. 3D map: In a focused period's panel, click View 3D Map for a rotating globe of that age. You can spin it and use the time slider to adjust the time inside of that period.
